Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this mod in my modpack?

Unless the modpack is available through curse/curseforge ONLY you may not bundle this mod or its dependencies.

Can I port this mod to X version?

No.

Will you make a 1.7.10 version?

No, unless you comission it to be done by SoggyMustache (the programmer of the mod) it will not happen.

My game crashed (or something isnt right)! What do I do???

Once you remove all mods except for ZAWA and Bookworm, join our discord and let an Admin know you have an issue.

Why are the spawns so weird and broken?

As of version 1.7.0 of ZAWA this should not be an issue, however if you still have problems there is a config file you can change to edit spawns.

How do I get an animal out of the ATV?

Shift + Right Click.

Will you add this animal???? Please I love it so much add it.

We are not taking any animal suggestions as our planned animals list is complete. However we do have animal votes every now and then.

I have a non-animal suggestion!

If you talk about something you'd like to see in zawa on our Discord fan chat we may consider adding it.
