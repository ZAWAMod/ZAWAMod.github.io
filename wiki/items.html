Wiki

Information about the mod

Only items that have a 'significant' feature are shown

Breeding Vials

The breeding vials can be used to breed two tamed ZAWA animals, to find out what vial the animal will require use the Data Book.

Animal Transport Vehicle

The A.T.V. can either be used as a standard vehicle or you can interact click the vehicle with a animal net to transport the animal in the net around in style.

Animal Ball

A basic enrichment toy that all animals in ZAWA can use.

Animal Fur/Leather

Most ZAWA animals will drop some fur or leather and some of them can be used to make armour

Brush

A basic enrichment item that can be used on almost every animal with fur

Animal Care Guide

A simple in game wiki that describes some of the things you have to do in order to keep animals happy and healthy

Cart

Can be used on a tamed Gaur to start riding it

Coin

Form of currency that ZAWA NPCs will accpet

Command Wand

The command wand can be used to make tamed animals go to your current location. By hitting the animal with the command wand you will select it and by right clicking the ground you will make the animal attempt to move to your location.

Animal Data Book

The animal data book can be used to get all the information you would possibly need about a zawa animal, such as its kibble, breeding vial, current stats, and more

Exploration Guide

The exploration guide will allow you to see all the animals that can be spawned in the biome that you select as well as the change, minimum, and maximum spawn rate

Ranged Weapons

The shotgun and hunting rifle can be used to deal damage while the tranquilizer gun can be used to either make the animal unable to move or poison them

Tracking Device

The tracking device can be used to find the location of your tamed animals within a 50 block radius of your current location

Zoo Painting

The zoo painting can be placed like a normal painting, however if you sneak and interact click near the painting (within around 1 block) with a zawa spawn egg the painting will change to that animal

Kibble

Kibbles can be used to tame ZAWA animals however depending on the trait of the animal it may take more than one try, use the animal data book to find out what kibble the animal you are trying to tame needs.
