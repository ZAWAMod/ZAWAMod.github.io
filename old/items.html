Only items with "special" abilities or signifigant uses are shown

There are more than these few items in the game!

Animal Net

Used to capture and release animals from any mod or mineraft

Ball

Basic animal toy used to keep enrichment up

Brush

Basic enrichment item used to keep enrichment up: works on ZAWA animals with fur

Cart

Adds a cart to a tamed Gaur to ride it

Coin

Currency that ZAWA NPC's will accept to buy items

Command Wand

Can be used on tamed ZAWA animals to make them move to your location

Data Book

Can be used on any ZAWA animal to get more information on the animal

Breeding Vial

One for each type of kibble, can be used for taming

Zoo Painting

A painting of ZAWA animals, sneak right click with a spawn egg by the painting to change it

Animal Kibble

Can be used on non-tamed ZAWA animals to tame them

Tracking Device

Can be used to see ZAWA animals within 100 blocks of your location

Tranquilizer Gun

A gun that can be used to make animals stop moving
